Beef and broccoli

A restaurant-style dish with full flavor

  • Prep Time: 15min
  • Cook Time: 15min
  • Additional Time: 30min
  • Total time: 1hr
  • Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup oyster sauce
  • 1/3 cup sherry
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 3/4lb beef round steak, cut inti 1/8in strips
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 thin slice of ginger root
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1lb broccoli

Steps to Prepare the dish

  1. Whisk sherry, soy sauce, cornstarch, sesame oil, sugar, and oyster sauce in a bowl until the sugar is dissolved
  2. Pour the mixture prepared in step one over the steak; cover and marinate for 30min
  3. Heat vegetable oil over med-high heat. Add ginger and garlic to flavor oil; remove after 1min and discard. Add broccoli to oil and stir or toss for 5-7min. Remove broccoli and set aside
  4. Add oil if needed, then add the beef with the marinade. Stir until sauce thickens and meat is not pink. Approx 5 min. Return broccoli with the meat until heated. Approx 3min.
  5. Now enjoy!
